Report on cabin resources

At the end of your stay you will be sent a form to fill out. This is so we know how when to check on equipment and refill on wood and gas for future trips.
While you are there, please make yourself aware of:

  • Does the smoke detector work?
  • How much wood is left?
  • How much gas is left?
